Welcome to Peak Runner

At Peak Runner, performance meets partnership. Whether you're chasing a sprint PB or building endurance, we help you train smarter, run stronger, and never go it alone.

Peak Runner's consultation

Initial Consultation to determine your level of fitness which will enable us to match you to your running .

Bespoke Fitness Regime

Depending on your personal targets, PeakRunner can provide you with nutritional advice an exercise program to work on your core, conditioning, strength, stamina and form.

S&C

Strength and Conditioning will make you stronger, faster and is highly recommended for those that are interested in building speed, stamina and improving muscle tone.

Bands

Bands are resistant tools that are useful as they help strengthen your core and build muscle tone.

Nutrition

A nutritional diet and hard work will help you reach your health goals. Diet Plans available on request.

Free Consultation

Where we can discuss your needs and set achievable targets.

Certified Trainer

One to one session which will result in a bespoke training program.

Flexible Time

Peak Runner will coordinate calendars and endeavour to find you a suitable running partner with similar fitness levels.
